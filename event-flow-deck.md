---

**Lead:** Crystal

- Develop the event concept, mood, and creative direction

---

# Lock Dates & Budget

- Set budget ceiling and spending authority

# Stage 3: The Invite

---

<!-- layout: two_column -->

# The Invite

::: left
**Info needed before design:**
- Event name, date, time
- Venue address
- Dress code
- RSVP method
- Dietary collection
- Tier list (VIP / general / press)

::: right
**Flow:**
- Collect all info first
- Crystal designs invites by tier
- Send invites
- Track RSVPs

**Lead:** Crystal designs, Dione collects info

# Stage 4: Merch & Gift Bags

---

<!-- layout: two_column -->

# Merch & Gift Bags

::: left
**Flow:**
- Pick default merch from catalog
- Confirm quantities per tier (A / B / C)
- Factory packing
- Ship to venue

**Lead:** Crystal selects, Andrea executes

::: right
**Watch out:**
- Allow packing time at factory
- Sea freight takes 2-3 weeks
- Ship date must be realistic
- Notify destination warehouse BEFORE shipping

**Key output:** Gift bags packed and shipped

# Stage 5: Vendors & Production

---

# Vendors & Production
**Lead:** Rachana/Britt (logistics), Crystal (creative vendors)

- Lock F&B — one decision-maker, one deadline
- Confirm furniture, signage, AV, print
- Set design freeze date and communicate to everyone
- Artwork deadline = printer deadline minus 48 hours

**Key output:** All vendors confirmed, print in production, design freeze passed

# Stage 6: Event Day

---

# Event Day — Top 5
**Lead:** Momo (ops), Crystal (creative)

- **1.** Walk floor plan and verify every station before doors open
- **2.** 10-min team huddle: roles, schedule, escalation path
- **3.** ONE person designated for real-time approvals
- **4.** Content team filming per shot list + spontaneous
- **5.** Collect ALL raw footage onto shared drive before leaving

# Stage 7: Post-Event Content

---

<!-- layout: two_column -->

# Post-Event Content

::: left
**Flow:**
- Raw footage uploaded within 24 hours
- Editor briefed with references + "what good looks like"
- Content rollout over 2-4 weeks
- Don't dump everything in one week

**Lead:** Crystal (direction), Dione (execution)

::: right
**Also:**
- Chase editor daily until deliverables are in
- Quality gate: review ALL edits before posting
- Invoices and receipts closed within 1 week
- Budget reconciliation (actual vs planned)

**Key output:** Content calendar live, budget reconciled

# 5 Rules That Apply Every Time

---

# The 5 Rules

- **One decision-maker per category** — not committee. One person decides, informs the rest.
- **Design freeze is sacred** — no new requests after the date. Communicate to everyone including founders.
- **Ship merch early** — never hand-carry through customs again.
- **Brief editors with references** — deliverables + turnaround + "what good looks like."
- **Schedule changes = communicate within 24 hours** — everyone whose work depends on it needs to know immediately.
